Ferrari 250 GTO (1962)

The Ferrari 250 GTO is one of the most iconic sports cars ever made. With only 36 units produced, it is a collector's holy grail.

Bugatti La Voiture Noire (2019)

The Bugatti La Voiture Noire is the most expensive new car ever made, costing $18.68 million. With its 8.0L quad-turbocharged W16 engine, it’s capable of reaching speeds exceeding 260 mph, blending technology, luxury, and performance into an automotive masterpiece.

$18,680,000

Rolls-Royce Sweptail (2017)

The Rolls-Royce Sweptail is a unique one-off luxury vehicle commissioned by a private client. Crafted by the bespoke team at Rolls-Royce, it combines elegance with advanced engineering, featuring a striking design and luxurious details that reflect the owner’s personal style.

$13,000,000

Mercedes-Benz 540K Special Roadster (1936)

The Mercedes-Benz 540K Special Roadster (1936) is a true icon of automotive luxury and engineering. Known for its elegance and performance, the 540K boasts a powerful 5.4-liter engine and exquisite design, combining both luxury and speed. It remains one of the most sought-after classics in the world.

$9,000,000
